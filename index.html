About Us

Essel Transports & Logistics (ETL) provides reliable, safe and comfortable vehicle rental services for individuals, corporate clients and events. We pride ourselves on timely service, well-maintained vehicles, and professional drivers to make every trip smooth.

Who We Are

Founded in 2020, Essel Transports & Logistics has grown to become one of Ghana's leading vehicle rental service providers. With over a decade of experience, we understand what our customers need: reliability, comfort, and professionalism.

Our fleet includes sedans, SUVs, minibuses, and luxury coaches—all regularly maintained to the highest standards. Every vehicle is insured and equipped with GPS tracking for your peace of mind.

Our Core Values

  • Safety: Your safety is our top priority. All drivers are screened and trained.
  • Reliability: We deliver on time, every time.
  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction drives everything we do.
  • Excellence: We maintain high standards in service quality and vehicle condition.

Our Mission

To deliver dependable transport solutions that make travel easy and stress-free for our customers, whether for business or leisure.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted transport partner in West Africa by providing modern vehicles, exceptional service, and professional expertise.

Why Choose Us

Competitive rates, 24/7 customer support, modern fleet, experienced drivers, and flexible booking options tailored to your needs.
